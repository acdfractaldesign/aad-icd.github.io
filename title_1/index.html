Dancing Rings

The base idea of the design is creating ring geometries which tansform depending on the parameters of the inner radius, outer radius and rotation. While looping through the geometry.parameters, the values of the innerRadius, outerRadius and the sum of both parameters define the xyz position of every ring. The idividual rotation of each ring is based on its list index value in the ring list and the rotation parameter. The rings can be scaled by a z factor. The aim of the design is to create a huge variety of geometric constellations with only a small change in parameters.
